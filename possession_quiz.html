This is a quiz on inalienable possession (mo, do, etc....) that affects words in three different ways depending on the category: Lenitables, Non-Lenitables and Vowels. This test is very much in beta and has not been thoroughly tested yet. Feel free to give it a go - if you identify any bugs or mistakes e-mail me at arelennox@gmail.com.
