Welsh or Y Gymraeg is a Brittonic language native to Wales. I became interested in Welsh after speaking to a few lovely native Welsh speakers and realizing the similarity of experience with other minority languages, such as Gaelic. I took a short 20-hour course generously paid for by LearnWelsh and have a great deal of respect for this beautiful language.

Please note that I am not a proficient Welsh speaker by any means. I make every effort to check my quizzes for errors, but I encourage you to check my quizzes against dictionaries or other resources yourself, and please contact me at arelennox@gmail.com if you have corrections or suggestions.
