Finnish is the most widely language spoken in Finland and part of the Uralic language family.

I first became interested in Finnish as the home of many of my favourite heavy metal bands (Sonata Arctica, Turisas, Ensiferum...), then later through a bunch of great Finnish people I met at university and gigs. I've visited Helsinki, Tampere and Joensuu and I love making karjalanpiirakka. One day I would love to be fluent in Finnish, though I still have a long way to go.

Please note that I am not a proficient Finnish speaker by any means. I make every effort to check my quizzes for errors, but I encourage you to check my quizzes against dictionaries or other resources yourself, and please contact me at arelennox@gmail.com if you have corrections or suggestions.
