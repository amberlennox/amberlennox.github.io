Hello! Welcome to my little corner of the web.

I'm a Scottish developer slash scientist, currently chasing my PhD in Physics in the US of A. My focus is on Cosmology, studying the Cosmic Microwave Background (CMB), AKA: baby pics of the universe! I get to work with telescopes, observe the earliest measurable light emitted after the Big Bang, and analyze the heck out of it!

I'm not just a physics nerd, though - I'm also a language geek. I struggled with French in school, but I struggled a lot with pronunciation and this gave me the (false!) impression that I was bad at languages. Later in life I look Gaelic lessons, and I fell in love with the culture, community and identity that is produced through language. Now I'm a proud learner of Gaelic and a big advocate language learning, particularly for minority languages. I found gameification apps, like Duolingo, really helped with my learning style. Since I like building digital creations, I experimented with some cool interactive quizzes to help with vocabulary, which you can check out on this website.

When I'm not nerding out over physics or languages, I'm snapping pics of birds in nature. You can find some of my favorite shots on this website. I love playing ice hockey, swimming and climbing too.

Oh, and did I mention I used to be in a folk metal band? Yup, I even founded a music festival back in the day!

This website is my passion project, and it's all free for you to enjoy. Just give me a shout if you find anything useful, and if you're feeling generous, make a donation to Refuweegee. Cheers!
