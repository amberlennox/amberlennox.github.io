Months Quiz










Enter the month corresponding to the number/picture shown.
A cheat sheet is down below.
(Month names are accepted with and without the definite article.)





























Pictures were chosen to correspond roughly to Bàrdachd nam Mìosan/The Months in Verse, which you may find a useful resource to practice with.
