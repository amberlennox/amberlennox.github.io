Korean is the official language of both North and South Korean and is spoken by approximately 80 million people in both countries as well as other parts of the world. Korean is typically written in Hangul, a writing system developed in the 15th century. I have prioritized giving answers in the Hangul script in my quizzes and I reccomend installing a Hangul keyboard before using them, though romanized (latin alphabet) responses are also given and accepted.

Please note that I am not a proficient Korean speaker by any means. I make every effort to check my quizzes for errors, but I encourage you to check my quizzes against dictionaries or other resources yourself, and please contact me at arelennox@gmail.com if you have corrections or suggestions.
