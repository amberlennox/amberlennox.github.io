Welcome to my webpage.

My name is Amber and I am a cosmologist currently working on both observation and analysis of the cosmic microwave background (CMB) in collaboration with telescopes such as SPIDER, BICEP/Keck and the South Pole Telescope (SPT).

I was born in Scotland and completed an undergraduate Master's degree in Theoretical Physics at the University of Glasgow in 2016. I've worked various on various scientific projects ranging from particle physics to dark matter detection in the United Kingdom, Germany and the United States.

I spend my free time playing ice hockey and learning Gaelic.

Fàilte gu an duilleag-lìn agam.

Is mise Amber, agus 's e cosmeòlaiche a th'annam. Tha mi ag obair aig SPIDER a' coimhead airson pòlarachadh 'B-modes' anns an Cùl-rèidiachd Mhion-thonnach Chosmach (CMC) le teileasgopan. Mar faigh sinn e, bidh e an innis dhuinn air a' chruinne-cè òg, 's leigidh e ris dhuinn a bheil bha 'inflation' ann.

'S ann Alba à tha mi agus dh'crìochnaich mi ceum nam fiosaigs aig Oilthigh Ghlaschu ann an 2016. Tha mi air air iomadh rudan a obair, mar eisimpleir fiosaigeachd nam bloighean agus damhna dorcha, anns an Rìoghachd Aonaichte, a' Ghearmailt agus na Stàitean Aonaichte.
