I'm Amber Lennox. I'm a cosmologist, Gaelic enthusiast and amateur ice hockey player.

I do research on the Cosmic Microwave Background (CMB). I completed an undergraduate Master's degree in Theoretical Physics at the University of Glasgow, I'm in the process of completing a PhD in Physics at the University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign.

Aside from the fact I swim, climb and skate, I'm a bit of a "basement nerd". I love programming unnecessary personal webpages, playing DnD, and listening to heavy metal. When I'm feeling a bit more serious, I'm enthusiastic about preserving minority languages, sustainability and the intersection of science and culture.

This page is just a collection of things I thought were interesting enough to share with the world.
